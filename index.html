About Me

I am a Graphic Designer whose work is primarily focused on editorial design and image manipulation. I have a strong background in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign which has allowed me to tackle a broad range projects with confidence. My military background has taught me to be competitive in any environment and work hard under any circumstance. As a load crew chief in the Air Force, I honed my managerial and interpersonal skills like team building, expediting and public speaking.

  4. Selected to be spokesman for various teams that I have been assigned to
